ordinary MNL bandit

Products are numbered from 1 by default. 0 is for non-purchase. It is assumed that the abstraction parameter of non-purchase is 1.

Input:

abspar: abstraction parameters of products revenue: revenue of products K: the cardinality upper bound of every assortment
